# Grounding pack — EU funding, grants & where to find the money (Track 07)

Real programmes only. Treat all amounts/deadlines as "check the current call" — they change.
Never invent contacts. Point learners at official portals and named agencies below.

## The EU-level money (who funds clean energy projects)
- **Modernisation Fund** — EU fund (financed by ETS allowances) for 10 lower-income member states
  incl. **Czechia**. Big channel for RES, grids, storage, efficiency. In CZ it is **operated by
  SFŽP ČR** via national programmes (e.g. RES+ for new renewables). *The single most relevant pot
  for Czech solar/BESS.*
- **Innovation Fund** — EU fund (also ETS-financed) for first-of-a-kind / large-scale clean tech &
  innovative renewables/storage. Competitive EU-wide calls. Bigger, more innovative projects.
- **Recovery and Resilience Facility (RRF)** — post-COVID money disbursed via each country's
  **National Recovery Plan**. In CZ: **Národní plán obnovy** (coordinated by MPO / Ministry of
  Finance), with green-energy components.
- **InvestEU** — EU guarantee mobilising private investment; delivered through the **EIB Group**
  and other implementing partners. More debt/guarantee than grant. Has an **Advisory Hub**.
- **Horizon Europe** — R&D&I grants (incl. energy R&D, demonstration). Via the **EU Funding &
  Tenders Portal**.
- **Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) – Energy** — cross-border energy infrastructure (PCIs).
- **European Investment Bank (EIB)** — the EU's bank; project loans, green/climate finance,
  advisory. Often the senior lender behind big renewable deals.
- **LIFE Programme** — smaller grants for environment & clean-energy transition (capacity building).

## The Czech delivery layer (where a CZ project actually applies)
- **SFŽP ČR** (Státní fond životního prostředí ČR) — operates Modernisation Fund programmes
  (incl. **RES+** for new solar/RES, and storage/efficiency programmes). First stop for CZ RES grants.
- **MPO** (Ministerstvo průmyslu a obchodu) — runs **OP TAK** (Operační program Technologie a
  aplikace pro konkurenceschopnost, 2021–27, ERDF-funded) with energy-saving & RES-for-business calls.
- **API** (Agentura pro podnikání a inovace) — the agency that administers OP TAK calls for firms.
- **CzechInvest** — investment & business-development agency; helps investors navigate incentives.
- **Nová zelená úsporám (New Green Savings)** — SFŽP programme, mainly buildings/rooftop + storage.

## Where to actually look (real portals)
- **EU Funding & Tenders Portal** — `ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders` — all centrally-managed EU
  calls (Innovation Fund, Horizon, LIFE, CEF). Register, search "Search Funding & Tenders".
- **Modernisation Fund** — `modernisationfund.eu` (EU overview) + national operator sites.
- **SFŽP ČR** — `sfzp.cz` — Czech Modernisation Fund / RES+ / Nová zelená úsporám calls.
- **OP TAK / API** — `agentura-api.org` / `mpo.cz` — business energy calls in CZ.
- **InvestEU Advisory Hub** / **EIB Advisory** — `advisory.eib.org` — project-prep support.
- **National Recovery Plan (CZ)** — `planobnovycr.cz`.
- National **promotional banks** (in CZ: **NRB — Národní rozvojová banka**) co-finance/guarantee.

## How the money actually works (teach this)
- **Grant vs loan vs guarantee**: grants don't repay (but are competitive, capped %, with
  state-aid limits); EIB/InvestEU/NRB give cheap debt or guarantees; most deals **stack** them.
- **Co-financing**: grants usually fund only part (e.g. 30–65%) of eligible capex; you bring the rest.
- **State-aid rules**: EU caps subsidy intensity (GBER / regional aid maps). A grant can't make a
  project "too profitable" — it's sized to close a viability gap.
- **Calls & windows**: money is released in **calls for proposals** with deadlines & eligibility.
  Miss the window, wait for the next. Always check the live call text.
- **Eligibility basics**: project location, technology, applicant type (SME vs large), readiness
  (permits, grid connection), and not having started works before applying.

## How a salesperson uses funding in a pitch
A grant or cheap EIB loan **improves the customer's payback and your own project IRR** — it's a
selling point ("part of the capex can be grant-funded under the Modernisation Fund, improving your
return"). It also **de-risks the build**, which later supports a cleaner exit.

## Where to find people / partners (real categories, not fake names)
- The **national operator** (SFŽP ČR / API) runs info days & has programme officers — public contacts.
- **EU/EIB advisory hubs** give free project-preparation help.
- **Grant consultancies** & **CzechInvest** help structure applications.
- Industry bodies: **Solární asociace** (CZ solar association), **SolarPower Europe**, **EASE**
  (storage). Good for events, contacts, and call intelligence.
