Learning objective

By the end you can name the official portals where live EU and Czech energy funding calls are published, point a client to the right kind of contact, and weave funding into a pitch — always with the discipline of verifying current details on the official site.

Where the live calls actually live

Every programme in this track publishes its open calls on an official portal. These are the authoritative sources — not a blog, not a half-remembered figure, not a third-party aggregator. When you need to know what is open today, you go to the portal that owns that programme.

For centrally-managed EU calls — Innovation Fund, Horizon Europe, LIFE, CEF — the home is the EU Funding & Tenders Portal, where you register and search. For the Czech delivery layer, each operator runs its own site: SFŽP for the Modernisation Fund programmes, the API and MPO sites for OP TAK, and the National Recovery Plan site for RRF-linked measures. Below is the directory; treat the URLs as starting points and always confirm details live.

Key term · EU Funding & Tenders Portal

The European Commission's single portal (ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders) for all centrally-managed EU calls — Innovation Fund, Horizon Europe, LIFE and CEF among them. You register, then use "Search Funding & Tenders" to find open calls and their documents. It is the authoritative source for EU-level calls; national programmes have their own operator sites.

Who to contact

Portals tell you what is open; people help you get through it. These are real categories of contact — use them as types, never invent a name or an email.

  • National operator programme officers — SFŽP ČR and API run info days and publish official contact points for their calls. They answer eligibility and process questions for the programmes they operate.
  • EIB / InvestEU advisory hubs — the InvestEU Advisory Hub and EIB Advisory offer free project-preparation support, especially where debt or guarantees are in play.
  • CzechInvest — the investment and business-development agency helps investors navigate Czech incentives and connect to the right programmes.
  • Grant consultancies — private firms that structure and write applications; useful when a project is complex or stacks multiple instruments.
  • Industry bodies — Solární asociace (the Czech solar association), SolarPower Europe, and EASE (the European storage association) are good for events, networks and intelligence on upcoming calls.

Find the actual person and their current contact details through the official channel each time — programme officers rotate, and contact points are published (and updated) on the operator's own site.

Always verify on the official site

Make this a reflex. Everything in this track — programmes, operators, portals, contact types — is durable. But the specifics that a client will act on are not: open calls, deadlines, budgets, eligible costs, aid intensities and named contacts all change. Before you quote a figure, promise a deadline, or send a client to a person, confirm it on the relevant official portal in the directory above.

Key term · "Check the current call"

The governing rule of this whole track. Any amount, deadline, eligibility threshold or contact you recall may be out of date. The authoritative version is the live call text on the official portal at the moment you apply. Verifying it protects the client, the application, and your own credibility.

In the room

How to weave funding into a client pitch — confidently, but without overpromising:

Sales script

Beyond the savings the system delivers on its own, there may be EU funding that improves your return. For a Czech project the likely route is the Modernisation Fund through the State Environmental Fund, and we would check the live call on their official site before putting any number on it. Part grant, part cheap finance, the rest your own — and the grant has to clear eligibility and the state-aid limits. So the offer is simple: a project that stands on its economics today, with funding as real upside we pursue properly. Shall we pull the current calls together?

Objections you will hear

Can you send me the exact grant amount and deadline right now?
Rebuttal

I can show you exactly where to find it — the official portal for the programme — and we will read the live call together. I will not quote a figure from memory, because budgets and deadlines change between calls, and a stale number would mislead you. The authoritative answer is always the current call text.

A consultant gave me a contact name months ago — should I just use that?
Rebuttal

Verify it before you rely on it. Programme officers rotate and contact points get updated, so the safe move is to confirm the current contact on the operator's official site. The categories of help are stable — national operators, EIB advisory, CzechInvest, industry bodies — but the specific person should always be checked live.

Do I really need funding, or is this just a sales angle?
Rebuttal

The project should make sense without it — that is the honest test. Funding is upside: a grant or cheap EIB loan can improve your payback and de-risk the build, which also supports a cleaner exit later. We pursue it properly through the right portal, but we never make it the reason the project exists.

SFŽP ČR operates the Czech Modernisation Fund programmes, so its site (sfzp.cz) is where those calls live.
